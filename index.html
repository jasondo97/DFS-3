"Green Threads" is an educational game that immerses players in the dynamic world of the fashion industry, focusing on its impact on the environment and the economy. Players take on the role of fashion investors, each starting with an initial amount of money to build their own fashion empire(stores, factories, properties). They navigate a board representing a variety of fashion brands, from fast fashion to luxury and sustainable labels. As players build their empire, they must also manage environmental taxes and carbon credits, which reflect the real-world consequences of fashion production and consumption. The game aims to educate players about the environmental effects of their choices. Players who invest in fast fashion brands may face higher environmental taxes, while those who opt for sustainable brands can earn carbon credits, giving them a strategic advantage. Additionally, players who overspend recklessly not only risk economic downfall but also increase their environmental impact, emphasizing the importance of responsible spending and sustainability in fashion. The ultimate goal is to create a successful, environmentally conscious fashion empire while balancing profits and sustainability.
